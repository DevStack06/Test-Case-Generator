Testcase Generator

Hey geeks! It is basically used to generate testcases on the basis of inputs provided and have many other features along with that. It works on arrays, numbers, strings, matrix(char & arrays), trees & graphs(weighted & unweighted).



Possible errors & FAQs

Possibly due to negative test-cases or negative/non-integer input lengths or also due to ranges interchange(minimum>maximum). Check the input again. Provide natural numbers for test-cases and the input lengths.

Distinctive field is chosen to ensure all the outputs are distinct(not common). Now, incase you provide insufficient number of characters and expect a larger distinct length output, you might possibly encounter this error. Provide input such that length of characters given is greater than or equal to the expected output length.

You encounter this in graphs sections. The edges is not possible with the given number of vertices. For valid inputs try providing Edges less than or = ((nodes)*(nodes-1))/2

They are usually to make sure whether the number of test-cases/ certain inputs provided, should be displayed as part of output or not.
